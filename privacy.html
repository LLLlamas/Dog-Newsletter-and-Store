Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Dogs & Llamas is a personal dog-sitting newsletter and product resource run by a husband-and-wife team — Lorenzo & Catalina Llamas. This page explains what information is collected, how it’s used, and how you can opt out any time.

Information Collected

If you subscribe to the newsletter, your email address is stored with the email platform used to send it (e.g., Mailchimp or beehiiv). No other personal data is collected by this website.

This site does not use tracking pixels, analytics scripts, advertising cookies, or any third-party trackers. There are no cookies set by this site directly. The schedule page stores your admin preferences in your own browser’s localStorage only — nothing is sent off your device.

How Your Email Is Used

  • To send the Dogs & Llamas newsletter (monthly at most)
  • To notify you of availability openings before they’re shared elsewhere
  • To share occasional subscriber perks and dog-care tips

Your email is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any other purpose.

Affiliate Links

The Curated Top Picks page contains Amazon affiliate links. If you click a link and make a purchase, this site may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This is disclosed on every page that contains affiliate links.

Clicking an affiliate link will take you to Amazon’s website, which has its own privacy policy and may set its own cookies. Dogs & Llamas has no control over Amazon’s data practices.

Rover Bookings

All dog-sitting bookings are handled through Rover.com and governed by Rover’s Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Dogs & Llamas does not store booking, payment, or review data — all of that lives on Rover.

Unsubscribing

You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time by replying to any email with the word “unsubscribe,” or by clicking the unsubscribe link in the footer of any newsletter. Your address will be removed promptly.

External Links

Links to Amazon, Rover, and other external sites open in a new tab with rel="noopener noreferrer" set for your security. This site is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of external sites.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach out on my Rover profile or reply to any newsletter email.

This is a personal, non-commercial newsletter. No legal boilerplate was generated by AI or auto-filled — this policy reflects actual practices.
