The Humans

Lorenzo & Catalina.

"We've been caring for dogs for a decade — ten years of pickups, long walks, and real photo updates. Dogs & Llamas started when our quiet little apartment became the unofficial hangout for everyone's pup."

Lorenzo handles pickups, walks along the Gantry waterfront, and the long weekend boarding stays. Catalina does daytime drop-ins, enrichment play indoors, and the dreaded post-walk wipe-down after a muddy park morning. We both reply within an hour, every time.

Home base is a 2nd-floor, air-conditioned apartment in Woodside — calm, cool, and a few steps from long avenues and nearby parks perfect for solo walks or for dogs who love meeting other pups. Twenty-five-plus dogs across Queens, Manhattan, and Westchester: chihuahuas, pit mixes, corgis, a French Bully who hated being alone, seniors with meds, puppies learning to potty, and a pair of large dogs who boarded together for two weeks over Christmas. Everyone leaves the same way — a little tired, a little happier.

Before You Book

Questions, Answered.

Morning walk around 8am (longer if it's cool out), breakfast, then couch-and-backyard time. Mid-day play or second walk. Dinner around 6pm. Evening walk and settle time. You'll get photo updates morning and evening, plus whenever something cute happens — which is often.
Yes, with a compatibility check. We've had two dogs boarded together for two weeks (Mango and Harley last Christmas — they became friends). If your dog doesn't love other dogs, we'll book solo days.
We follow Rover's flexible policy for Rover bookings: full refund if you cancel by 5pm the day before. For direct returning clients, we're more flexible — just let us know as early as you can so we can offer the spot to someone on the waitlist.
We're in Woodside, Queens — two blocks from the 61st St-Woodside 7/LIRR station. Pickup and drop-off at our home is easiest. For a small fee we can also pickup within ~2 miles (Sunnyside, Astoria, LIC, Jackson Heights).
Rover-booked stays are covered under the Rover Guarantee (up to $25k in vet coverage, premium insurance). For house-sitting, we carry a separate liability policy — happy to share on request.
We handle pills, injections (insulin included), elderly dogs, post-surgery care, and anxious pups. Walk us through the routine on the meet & greet. We log every dose and photograph it so you can verify.
