Spring openings + a quick tip for warmer-weather walks

April 2026 • Issue 01

Spring spots are opening up for weekends and weekday drop-ins through April and into May. If you have travel or a busy stretch coming up, it's a good time to reach out early.

This month's tip: as pavement heats up, aim for morning or evening walks. A quick test — if the sidewalk is too hot for the back of your hand for five seconds, it's too hot for paws.
