Our Story

Why we do this

We started caring for dogs on Rover because it combined two things we already loved — animals, and building real relationships with people in our community. What we didn’t expect was how deeply personal it would become. Each dog is completely different: Turbo needs his high-energy fetch sessions to settle down, Troy just wants to cruise on a long walk, Dakota’s a velcro dog who follows you room to room. Learning what each one needs is genuinely one of our favorite parts of the job.

We’re proud to be Rover Star Sitters — a designation Rover reserves for sitters with consistently 5-star reviews, fast response times, and repeat clients. It’s not a title we take lightly. Every stay, we try to earn it again.

We’re also currently working toward our dog training certification so we can bring even more to every stay — better handling, better communication, and a deeper understanding of what dogs are telling us. If your dog is working on anything specific (leash manners, recall, basic cues), let us know and we can build it gently into their routine.

We treat every stay as if it’s our own dog. That means regular photo updates, keeping to your dog’s routine, and messaging you when something funny or sweet happens — because those moments are the whole point.
