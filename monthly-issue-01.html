Dog Tip of the Month

As the weather warms up, pavement can heat quickly — even on days that don’t feel especially hot. A good rule of thumb: if you can’t hold the back of your hand on the sidewalk for five seconds comfortably, it’s too warm for your dog’s paws. Early morning or evening walks tend to be the most comfortable this time of year.
