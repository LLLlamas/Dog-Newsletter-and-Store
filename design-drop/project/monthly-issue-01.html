Dog tip of the month

The pavement test takes seven seconds.

Hot-paw check, one hand down.

Press the back of your hand to the sidewalk for seven seconds. If you can't hold it, your dog can't walk on it. Queens sidewalks hit 130°F in full sun by mid-June — shift walks to before 9am or after 7pm, and stick to the shady side of the street. Pretzel taught us this the hard way last summer.
