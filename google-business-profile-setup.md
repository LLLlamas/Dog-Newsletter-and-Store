# Google Business Profile + WhatsApp Business Setup Guide

> **Purpose:** Step-by-step instructions to set up a Google Business Profile and WhatsApp Business for Dogs & Llamas. This gets you into Google Maps, the local 3-pack ("dog boarding near me"), and gives clients a direct messaging channel.
>
> **Time needed:** ~30 minutes for both.
>
> **Reusable:** These same steps apply to any local service business. When setting up another project, follow the same flow with different business details.

---

## Part 1: Google Business Profile

### Why this matters
When someone in Woodside or Sunnyside searches "dog boarding near me," Google shows a map with 3 local businesses (the "local 3-pack"). Without a Google Business Profile, you're invisible in that section — no matter how good your website SEO is.

### Step-by-step setup

**1. Go to:** https://business.google.com/create

**2. Sign in** with `dogsandllamasservice@gmail.com` (same account as the site emails).

**3. Business name:** `Dogs & Llamas`
- Must match EXACTLY what's on your website. Google penalizes mismatches.

**4. Business category:**
- Primary: `Pet Boarding Service`
- Additional categories (add all that apply):
  - `Dog Walker`
  - `Dog Day Care Center`
  - `Pet Sitting Service`

**5. Do you want to add a location customers can visit?**
- Select **Yes** if you board dogs at your home (clients come to you for drop-off).
- Enter your home address. You can choose to **hide the exact address** and only show the service area — recommended for home-based businesses.

**6. Service area:**
Add each neighborhood as a separate area:
- `Woodside, Queens, NY`
- `Sunnyside, Queens, NY`
- `Astoria, Queens, NY`
- `Long Island City, Queens, NY`
- `Midtown Manhattan, NY`

**7. Contact information:**
- **Phone:** Your WhatsApp Business number (see Part 2 below)
- **Website:** `https://llllamas.github.io/Dog-Newsletter-and-Store/`
- **Email:** `dogsandllamasservice@gmail.com`

**8. Business hours:**
- Set your typical availability hours (e.g., 8 AM – 8 PM daily for boarding inquiries)
- Or mark as "By appointment" if you prefer

**9. Business description (750 char max):**
```
Professional dog boarding, daycare, walking, drop-in visits, and house sitting in Woodside, Sunnyside, Astoria, Long Island City, and Midtown Manhattan. Lorenzo & Catalina Llamas — Rover Star Sitters. Every stay includes walks, playtime, and photo updates. Book directly through our website for no platform fees. Serving western Queens and nearby neighborhoods. Mention code PUPS10 for 10% off your next booking.
```

**10. Photos — upload these from your media/ folder:**
- Profile photo: `media/dakota-and-me.jpg` (you + a dog = trust)
- Cover photo: `media/turbo-outside.jpg` (action shot, bright, eye-catching)
- Additional photos (upload 5-10):
  - `media/mango-and-harley-1.jpg`
  - `media/teddy-lying-down-with-kong-holding-hand.jpg`
  - `media/pretzel-sleeping-in-comfortable-bed.jpg`
  - `media/pumba-smiling-on-couch.jpg`
  - `media/athena-walk.jpg`
  - `media/zephyr-and-me-walk.jpg`
  - `media/ace-smiling-sitting-solo.jpg`

**11. Verify your business:**
- Google will send a postcard to your address with a verification code (takes 5-14 days)
- OR you may qualify for phone/email/video verification (faster)
- Until verified, your profile won't appear in search results

**12. After verification:**
- Add a **booking link**: `https://llllamas.github.io/Dog-Newsletter-and-Store/schedule.html`
- Add **products** (your services with pricing):
  - Overnight Boarding — $90/night
  - Doggy Day Care — $50/day
  - Drop-In Visits — $45/visit
  - Dog Walking — $25/walk
  - House Sitting — $100/night
- Ask 2-3 past clients to leave a **Google review** — reviews are the #1 ranking factor for local search

### Critical consistency rules
Google checks that your business info matches across your website, social media, and the profile. Keep these IDENTICAL everywhere:

| Field | Must match exactly |
|-------|-------------------|
| Business name | `Dogs & Llamas` |
| Address | Same city/state/zip everywhere |
| Phone | Same number on site + profile |
| Website URL | `https://llllamas.github.io/Dog-Newsletter-and-Store/` |
| Email | `dogsandllamasservice@gmail.com` |

## Part 2: WhatsApp Business

### Why WhatsApp Business (not regular WhatsApp)
- Free business profile with your business name, description, hours, and catalog
- Auto-replies ("Thanks for reaching out! Lorenzo will reply within 2 hours.")
- Labels to organize conversations (Leads, Booked, Past Clients)
- Works on the same phone number as regular WhatsApp (separate app)
- Clients see "Dogs & Llamas" instead of just a phone number

### Step-by-step setup

**1. Install WhatsApp Business** (separate app from regular WhatsApp):
- [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.whatsapp.w4b)
- [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/app/whatsapp-business/id1386412985)

**2. Register with your phone number.** You can use:
- Your existing personal number (it will migrate from regular WhatsApp)
- OR a separate business number (recommended if you want to keep personal and business separate)

**3. Set up your Business Profile:**
- **Business name:** `Dogs & Llamas`
- **Category:** `Pet Services`
- **Description:**
  ```
  Professional dog boarding, daycare, walking & house sitting in Woodside, Sunnyside & Queens NYC. Book directly — no platform fees. Rover Star Sitters.
  ```
- **Address:** Woodside, Queens, NY (same as Google Business)
- **Business hours:** Same as Google Business
- **Email:** `dogsandllamasservice@gmail.com`
- **Website:** `https://llllamas.github.io/Dog-Newsletter-and-Store/`

**4. Set up auto-reply messages:**

**Away message** (when outside business hours):
```
Hey! Thanks for reaching out to Dogs & Llamas. Lorenzo is away right now but will get back to you first thing. If you need to book, you can check open dates anytime at: https://llllamas.github.io/Dog-Newsletter-and-Store/schedule.html
```

**Greeting message** (first-time contacts):
```
Hi there! Welcome to Dogs & Llamas. I'm Lorenzo — I handle all the boarding, walking, and daycare personally. What can I help you with? If you want to check open dates, here's the calendar: https://llllamas.github.io/Dog-Newsletter-and-Store/schedule.html
```

**Quick replies** (saved responses you can send with one tap):
- `/available` → "Here's my current availability: [link to schedule]. Let me know what dates work for you!"
- `/pricing` → "Overnight boarding is $90/night, daycare is $50/day, walks are $25, drop-ins are $45. Book through the site and I'll confirm within 24 hours."
- `/thanks` → "Thanks so much for trusting us with [dog name]! If you ever want to rebook, mention PUPS10 for 10% off your next stay."

**5. Create labels** for conversation management:
- `New Lead` (just reached out)
- `Booked` (confirmed booking)
- `Awaiting Payment` (approved, not yet paid)
- `Past Client` (completed stay — follow-up target)

**6. Add your WhatsApp number to your website** (I've already prepped this — just share the number and I'll wire it in).

### WhatsApp click-to-chat link format
Once you have the number, the link format is:
```
https://wa.me/1XXXXXXXXXX?text=Hi%20Lorenzo%2C%20I%20found%20you%20on%20Dogs%20%26%20Llamas%20and%20I%27d%20like%20to%20ask%20about%20booking.
```
Replace `1XXXXXXXXXX` with your full number including country code (1 for US).

## Part 3: Connecting Everything

After both are set up:

1. **Add WhatsApp number to Google Business Profile** as the primary phone
2. **Add WhatsApp click-to-chat link to your website** footer + about page + schedule page
3. **Add the Google Business link to your website** (I'll add a "Find us on Google" badge)
4. **Submit your sitemap to Google Search Console:**
   - Go to https://search.google.com/search-console
   - Add your site URL
   - Submit `https://llllamas.github.io/Dog-Newsletter-and-Store/sitemap.xml`
   - This tells Google to crawl your pages faster
